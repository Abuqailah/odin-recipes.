Foul Recipe

An ancient Egyptian recipe made from fresh fava beans serve 3

Ingredients

  • 2 tbl cumin
  • 2 tbl black pepper
  • 1 kilo mashed fava beans
  • 2 diced tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 2 diced onions
  • 1 bag of flat bread
  • 1/2 kilo creamy feta cheese

    • Steps

    1. combine onions , garlic, tomatoes and olive oil in sauce pan until onions and garlic brown
    2. add mashed fava beans
    3. stir to mix contents n let simmer for 5 min
    4. serve with warm flat bread
