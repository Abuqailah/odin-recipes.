Cheese Burger

Old Fashioned Cheese Burger serves 5

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground beef
  • 1/2lb sliced American cheese
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 sliced tomato
  • 1/2 ts salt
  • 1/2 ts pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 green pepper
  • olive oil pan spray
  • 5 fresh burger buns
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet or griddle
  2. Combine ground beef onion , pepper , salt , green pepper and egg together in mixing bowl
  3. Form mixture into 5 patties
  4. Spray skittle with pan spray with light coat just to prevent sticking
  5. Add patties to skillet and let cook for 10 min on high heat
  6. Flip patties and repeat the previous step while lightly pressing on patties to your desired thickness
  7. Add slices of cheese to your liking while burgers are still cooking
  8. Add Mayo and Ketchup to buns
  9. Add burger to bun and enjoy
